About This Product

Our comprehensive formula utilizes multiple skin support technologies that target multiple signs of aging at once. With five well-studied peptide technologies, skin-friendly amino acids, and multiple hyaluronic acids, this serum helps improve the appearance of fine lines while supporting your skin's overall elasticity, smoothness and firmness.

Key Benefits

  • Targets advanced signs of aging
  • Improves skin elasticity & firmness
  • Provides antioxidant support
  • Combats dryness and environmental stress

Ingredients

Aqua (Water), Glycerin, Copper Tripeptide-1, Acetyl Hexapeptide-8, Palmitoyl Tripeptide-1, Palmitoyl Tetrapeptide-7, Hyaluronic Acid, Sodium PCA, urea, Allantoin, Panthenol, Arginine, Aspartic Acid, Glycine, Alanine, Serine, Valine, Isoleucine, Proline, Threonine, Histidine, Phenylalanine, Citric Acid, Sodium Citrate, Phenoxyethanol, Caprylyl Glycol.

How to Use

Apply a few drops to clean, dry skin in the morning and/or evening. Gently pat into face, neck, and décolletage until fully absorbed. Follow with moisturizer and sunscreen (AM).

For best results, use consistently as part of your daily skincare routine. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
